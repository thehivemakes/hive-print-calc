How to Estimate 3D Print Filament Usage

Most slicers show estimated print volume in cubic centimeters. Multiply by the filament density to get weight in grams. Divide by the spool weight to see what percentage of a spool the print uses.

Filament length can be calculated from weight: length (m) = weight (g) / (density × π × (d/2)²) where d is filament diameter in cm.
