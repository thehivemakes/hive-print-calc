The True Cost of 3D Printing

The cost of a 3D print goes beyond filament. Electricity, printer wear, failed prints, and your time all factor in. This calculator gives you the full picture.

Most hobbyists underestimate costs by 40-60% when they only count filament. Printer depreciation alone typically adds $0.10-0.30 per hour of printing.
