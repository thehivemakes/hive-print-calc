Choosing the Right Layer Height

Layer height is the single biggest factor in print quality and speed. Thinner layers produce smoother surfaces but take longer. The standard 0.4mm nozzle works well from 0.1mm to 0.3mm layers.

For miniatures and detailed models, use 0.08–0.12mm layers. For functional parts where appearance matters less, 0.2–0.3mm is a good balance. For rapid prototypes, go up to 75% of nozzle diameter.
